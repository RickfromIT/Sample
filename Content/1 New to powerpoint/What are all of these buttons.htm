What are all of these buttons?

When you open PowerPoint for the first time you will see the screen below.

Fig 1.2 PowerPoint Window

 

Fig 1.3 The Ribbon

 

In Fig 1.3 you can see the top portion of the window. This part is known at the ribbon. The Ribbon is split up into smaller sections known as "Tabs" for example "Home", "Design". The Home tab is the default tab that will be selected each time you open PowerPoint.

 

In PowerPoint you work with slides. The slide you are working with is displayed in the slide pane as shown in Fig 1.4.
