1 Introduction

The introduction slide is for you to introduce the educational expedience you went on.

  1. Insert an image on to the slide that represents the area you saw
  2. Right click on the image and select send to back
  3. Scroll to the left of the window until you see some text
  4. Replace this text with the area you visited, the dates of your trip, and the people who went on the trip

Once completed this slide will form the opening of your presentation. The image will form the background and the text will scroll across the screen from the right to the left.

 

Where you insert the picture for the background you could insert a small video file to play on a loop.
