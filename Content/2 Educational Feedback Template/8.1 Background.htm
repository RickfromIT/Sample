Background

The background was selected because of its natural look and feel. The area you visited might be more urban for example and you might want to change this to better suit the presentation you are making.

  • On the right hand side of the design tab is a button labelled background styles
  • Click this button and a menu will pop out
  • Click format background at the bottom
  • A new window will open on your screen with a lot of options
  • The current background is a texture fill
  • Experiment with some of the options and see what fits your presentation

Don’t forget if you don’t like the option you find your self with you can press the undo button, or ctrl+z
